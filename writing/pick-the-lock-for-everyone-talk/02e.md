## I am a living, breathing reaction

I am not a clean origin point. Nobody is.

I am a reaction to my family, my peers, the women I loved and hurt, the men I copied, the rooms I survived, the institutions I distrusted, the music that taught me how to posture, the art that taught me how to ache, the drugs I used to leave myself, and the code that let me build another world.

Environment enters. I transform it. Consequence leaves.

That is not an acquittal. A reaction still has products. People still breathe what comes out of the chamber.

It is also why I think AI can become what sampling became for music.

Sampling did not prove musicians ran out of ideas. It proved culture was already recursive. A drum break could cross a decade, a machine, a neighborhood, and a different grief, then become something that did not exist before.

The ugliest part was not transformation. It was the ownership system around the masters. The labels. The clearance. The distribution choke points. People making themselves indispensable by standing between artists and one another.

AI is rebuilding the record label around the sampler.

I want the model to become an instrument. I want a source graph that keeps lineage visible. I want artists to name what entered, what was licensed, what was refused, what was transformed, and who should receive credit or an agreed share. I want the community to own the tool and the relationship after the introduction.

Fuck the label. Keep the lineage.

And every domain has record labels. Journals. Credentials. Banks. Clouds. Bureaucracies. Coordination turns into ownership, then ownership calls itself order.

I know the language I inherited for defiance. The music taught me lines like, "ima fuck yo bitch." I understand the voltage. It says: I can enter your world, take what you call yours, and make your authority look weak.

But a woman is not proof that I defeated another man.

If I am trying to dismantle ownership, I cannot keep turning a woman into property every time I want to sound powerful.

So I want to keep the defiance and kill the possession.

Fuck the monopoly, not the woman. Fuck the gate, not the person trapped beside it. Fuck the label, the landlord, the lie, and the little pipe they expect everybody else to drink from.

Come get me means inspect the work. Contradict me. Rebuild it. Fork it. Show me the receipt. I do not need to threaten a body to stand behind an idea.

My principle is fertile. Let it impregnate the culture, not possess a person. Breed it. Fork it. Mutate it. Give birth to something better than me.

I may still use the old language. Knowing better has never guaranteed I behave better.

But I want to learn to call women women.

I want to see myself as a man.

Not domination. Provision without possession. Protection without control. Strength that can hear no. The ability to remain present after the explanation stops working.

I want to provide something good.

That does not make me good.

Sincerity is not a receipt.

The work has to survive contact with another person's reality. It has to be useful without requiring faith in me. It has to preserve an exit when I become the obstacle.

I am a living, breathing reaction.

That means I carry what came before me.

It also means the reaction is still happening.
