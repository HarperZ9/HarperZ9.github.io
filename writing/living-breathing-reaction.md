## Statement

I am not a clean origin point. Nobody is.

I am a living, breathing reaction: to my family, my peers, the women I loved and hurt, the men I copied, the rooms I survived, the institutions I distrusted, the music that taught me how to posture, the art that taught me how to ache, the drugs I used to leave myself, the code that let me build another world, and every person who showed me kindness when I had not yet learned how to return it.

Environment enters. I transform it. Consequence leaves.

That is not an acquittal. A reaction still has products. People still breathe what comes out of the chamber. It is also why I believe AI can become what sampling became for music.

Sampling showed that culture was already recursive. The ugliest part was not transformation itself. It was the ownership system around the masters: the labels, clearance regimes, distribution choke points, contracts, and people who made themselves indispensable by standing between artists and one another.

AI is rebuilding the record label around the sampler.

The model can become an instrument. A source graph can preserve lineage. Artists can declare what entered, what was licensed, what was refused, what was transformed, and who should receive credit or an agreed share. Communities can own the tools, relationships, and treasury. A steward can coordinate without becoming the landlord of the culture moving through the system.

Fuck the label. Keep the lineage.

I know the language I inherited for defiance. I also hear the property claim inside it. A woman is not evidence that I defeated another man. If I am trying to dismantle ownership, I cannot keep turning a woman into property whenever I want to sound powerful.

So I want to keep the defiance and kill the possession.

Come get me means: inspect the work. Contradict me. Rebuild it. Fork it. Show me the receipt.

My principle is fertile. Let it impregnate the culture, not possess a person. Breed it. Fork it. Mutate it. Give birth to something better than me.

I want to learn to call women women. I want to see myself as a man: provision without possession, protection without control, strength that can hear no, and the ability to remain present after the explanation stops working.

I want to provide something good.

That does not make me good. Sincerity is not a receipt. The work has to survive contact with another person's reality and remain useful without requiring faith in me.

I am a living, breathing reaction.

That means I carry what came before me.

It also means the reaction is still happening.
