Check AI-assisted work. Keep the trail.

WARDEN is a system for checking AI-assisted work. It records what was claimed, what evidence supports it, where the work came from, what changed, and what a human reviewer should look at before trust moves forward.

Workflow

What WARDEN does.

01
Claim review

It makes claims reviewable.

WARDEN turns a statement into something a reviewer can inspect: the claim, the evidence behind it, the source records, the changed files, and the open gaps. The immediate value is a shorter path from output to accountable review.

claimsevidencematurity labels
02
Reviewer handoff

It turns agent output into a handoff.

The workflow moves from surface inspection to provenance checks, evidence indexing, anomaly review, and human-readable reporting. It gives teams a record they can hand to another person without asking them to believe the process from memory.

Position

Accountability engine for AI-assisted work.

WARDEN is the body of work underneath the public proof tools: a way to make agent-assisted systems more inspectable, more honest about their limits, and easier to hand to a real reviewer. The theme is accountability: claims, evidence, provenance, anomalies, and human ownership in one line of sight.
