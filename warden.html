Position

Public leaves, private core, one theme.

WARDEN is useful when AI-assisted work needs a review trail: live-state observations, claims, evidence, provenance, anomalies, witness receipts, and human ownership in one line of sight. The public repos show pieces of that pattern; they do not ask the reader to accept the private core on faith.
