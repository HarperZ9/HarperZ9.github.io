A witness is not an authority.

EMET is the witness anchor in the proof-surface stack. It checks whether bytes and presented views still match what they claim to represent, then emits a restrained verdict instead of asking for trust.

No TRUSTED verdict

Immediate value as of June 15, 2026

Who uses it: reviewers, AI workflow builders, maintainers, and diligence teams who need a small external check on source/view consistency.

Use this when a repo, report, prompt, or generated view needs an external source/view consistency check.

What it does not claim: It reports a comparison; it does not decide whether a system is safe or trustworthy.
