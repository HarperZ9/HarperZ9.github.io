About the Studio and Project Telos

This is the live surface of Project Telos, the engine. Telos is the thesis made tangible: a person and a model stand before the same witnessed frame, generate from it, transform it, and verify every step together. Bring your own image, video, audio, OBJ, GLTF, PLY, CSV, or JSON; render Atelier studies, 2D and 3D fractals, nD geometry, sound-reactive visuals, physics discovery runs, and the First Integral showcase, a live Kepler orbit that fits its own conserved quantity and re-checks the result as MATCH, DRIFT, or UNVERIFIABLE; then export pixels, perception JSON, OBJ, GLTF, or witnessed artifacts. The right panel shows what the model is given: frame, fingerprint, colours, channels, receipts, and the five-flagship stack feeding the work. In the Showcase source a connected model receives the identical readout the scene announces aloud, so a person and a model read the same seed, invariant, and verdict.
