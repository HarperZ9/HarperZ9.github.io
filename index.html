The floor

Everything here is built on one finding: something that looks verified is not the same as the thing it is meant to guarantee. Proving who you are is not permission to act. A practice run on old data is not money earned. A certificate on the wall is not safety.

The fix is the same everywhere. Work the answer out yourself, from outside the thing making the claim. That is the floor every Project Telos engine stands on, and the point is what you build on top of it.
